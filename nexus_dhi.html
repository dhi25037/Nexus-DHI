Our Manifesto

Pledges to Connect and Transform

Together we can create meaningful change. Our pledges focus on empowering students, improving our learning environment, and fostering a vibrant community.

  • Host an official TEDx event at our school to share ideas and inspire innovation.
  • Introduce a student-led after‑school program and challenge semester where everyone can explore new interests.
  • Upgrade student ID cards with improved design and information, including a check-card feature for convenience.
  • Redesign our uniform jumper for comfort and style.
  • Provide blazer-style uniforms for formal occasions.
  • Establish a federation of clubs to strengthen collaboration and support across all student organisations.

Vision

Change Through Connection

Our vision is to foster a school where every voice is heard and everyone feels connected. By building bridges between students, teachers and the community, we will create an environment of trust, respect and collaboration.

Together, we will encourage new initiatives, celebrate diversity and cultivate a school culture that empowers everyone to contribute.
